## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Be Specific:** "Reduced load time by 40%" beats "Improved performance"
2. **Show Impact:** Focus on results, not just tasks
3. **Be Honest:** Only include what you can talk about in interviews
4. **Keep It Fresh:** Update regularly with new accomplishments
5. **Test Everything:** Click every link, verify every image
6. **Mobile First:** Many recruiters browse on phones
7. **Less is More:** Don't overcrowd‚Äîmake it scannable
8. **Personal Touch:** Show your personality, not just skills
9. **Call to Action:** Make it easy for people to reach you
10. **Backup First:** Save a copy before major changes
